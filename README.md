## Getting Started

In this program you start in a hallway facing a door. Use a and d to look around and w and s to walk forwards and backwards. Click on the door or press d to open it and close it. When you walk through the door you will be in a dark room. Click on the lamp or press l to turn on and off the lamp on the desk. Click the first button or press c to turn on or off the ceiling light. Walk over to the window and drag the blinds to the right (only works dragging right) to open the blinds. Press the second button or r to start or stop rain/snow. Click the third button or press w to toggle winter on or off. If it is winter the rain will turn to snow. Click the fourth button or press f to turn on or off fog.
